Open Remote Package Installer

Before you can install games, you need to launch Remote Package Installer on your PS4.

  1. 1 Make sure your PS4 is jailbroken (HEN / GoldHEN or equivalent)
  2. 2 From the PS4 home screen, open the Remote Package Installer app
  3. 3 The app will display "Waiting for packages…" — this means it's ready
  4. 4 Keep it open while you browse and install from this page
Both your PC and PS4 must be on the same Wi-Fi network.

Connect Your Console

Enter your PS4's IP address to connect. The status indicator will turn green when your console is reachable.

  1. 1 On your PS4 go to Settings → Network → View Connection Status to find the IP
  2. 2 Type the IP into the PS4 IP Address box in the top bar
  3. 3 The box turns green when your console is connected and RPI is open
Your PS4's IP is saved automatically — you only need to enter it once.

Link Real-Debrid

Real-Debrid unlocks fast, unrestricted download links for your games.

  1. 1 Visit real-debrid.com/apitoken and copy your API token
  2. 2 Paste it into the RD box in the top bar and press Enter
  3. 3 The box turns green when validated — you're linked!
Your token is stored only in your browser's local storage — never sent to any server.

You're All Set!

Everything is ready. Here's how to install your first game:

  1. 1 Browse the library and click View Details on any game
  2. 2 Choose a download link and click Send to PS4
  3. 3 Watch the Downloads panel (bottom-right) for live install progress
Use the Favourites and Installed filters to keep your library organised.
